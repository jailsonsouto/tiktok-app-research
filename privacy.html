Privacy Policy - Research-Tool-DSA

Effective Date: February 02, 2026

1. Scope of this Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Research-Tool-DSA" academic application processes data obtained via the TikTok for Developers API for the sole purpose of academic research in the cosmetics market.

2. Data Collected

The tool processes only publicly available TikTok data, such as:

  • Public video metadata (e.g., captions, hashtags, like/share counts).
  • Public comments and engagement metrics.

We do not collect:

  • Private messages or non‑public content.
  • User login credentials or passwords.
  • Sensitive personal identifiers used to directly identify individuals.

3. Purpose and Legal Basis

Data is processed exclusively for:

  • Academic Netnography and Sentiment Analysis (ABSA/ASTE) of the cosmetics market.
  • Statistical modeling and Natural Language Processing experiments.

No data is used for targeted advertising, user profiling for commercial purposes, or resale.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored in a secure research environment and retained only for the duration necessary to complete the MBA research project and related academic validation. After this period, data may be deleted or irreversibly anonymized and aggregated.

5. Data Sharing

Individual‑level data is not shared with third parties. Only anonymized and aggregated research results (e.g., charts, statistics, sentiment distributions) may be shared in academic contexts such as thesis documents, presentations, or scientific publications.

6. Compliance with TikTok Policies

The project complies with TikTok’s Developer Terms and related policies as outlined on TikTok for Developers . If TikTok’s policies change, the usage of the API and this tool will be reviewed and may be adjusted or discontinued.
